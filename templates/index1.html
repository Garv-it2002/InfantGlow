Child Health Status Prediction

This pioneering project leverages weight-to-age data to predict and communicate the health status of infant children. By analyzing these metrics in relation to their age and diet, the project offers valuable insights for parents and caregivers. Furthermore, the inclusion of a helpline for doctors ensures expert guidance in interpreting and acting upon the results. This comprehensive approach not only enhances parental awareness but also equips healthcare professionals with targeted strategies for effective intervention and recovery, promising improved health outcomes for these young ones.
